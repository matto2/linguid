# LiNGUiD French Content Acquisition Plan

Last updated: 2026-05-13

## Goal

Build the first 12 hours of high-quality French video content for LiNGUiD, with a bias toward beginner-friendly, slow, clear speech. The starting mix should be practical, rights-aware, and resilient to low response rates from creators and studios.

Operator files:

- `docs/content-acquisition-targets.csv` - prioritized target list, contact routes, asks, and next actions.
- `docs/content-outreach-kit.md` - email, DM, follow-up, positive-response, and negative-response scripts.
- `docs/pilot-content-license.md` - one-page draft pilot license for hosted clips.
- `docs/content-upload-sop.md` - rights statuses, hosted upload flow, YouTube embed import spec, QA, and 14-day execution plan.

Target mix:

- 8 hours of official YouTube embeds from public, embeddable videos.
- 2 hours of creator-approved embeds with LiNGUiD-created captions or supplied transcripts.
- 2 hours of fully licensed hosted clips where the creator explicitly grants permission.

Do not download or rehost YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, or TV content unless permission is explicit. Do not scrape captions without permission. Use official embeds by default.

Fastest safe starting point: Easy Languages explicitly says its YouTube videos may be embedded in courses/curricula without obtaining permission. Use that for the first embed-only French batch, while still reaching out for caption/transcript permission before enabling LiNGUiD tap-to-translate captions.

## Strategy

Lead with Julian as a person in direct outreach, not as a corporate partnerships desk.

Parrot-like strategy, adapted safely:

- Copy the user experience pattern: real short videos, low-friction discovery, captions, saved vocabulary, and creator attribution.
- Do not copy any rights shortcuts. LiNGUiD should use official embeds, written caption permission, and signed hosting licenses.
- Treat creators as acquisition partners, not raw supply. The pitch is "I want to send learners back to you," not "I want free content."
- Build the first catalog from sources that already want to teach French, then add kids/studio content after the app has a credible public site and a clean permission trail.

If a creator responds negatively:

- Thank them.
- Confirm LiNGUiD will not use or rehost their content without permission.
- Ask whether official YouTube embedding only is acceptable.
- If still no, ask who else makes clear beginner French content they respect.

## Pilot License Defaults

Use email permission for official embeds and transcript/caption rights. Use a one-page signed pilot license for hosted files.

Default pilot license terms:

- Non-exclusive.
- Worldwide.
- 12 months.
- Free pilot.
- Creator retains ownership.
- LiNGUiD may host and display approved clips inside the app.
- LiNGUiD may create captions, transcripts, vocabulary metadata, and difficulty labels for app learning features.
- No AI model training on creator content.
- No sublicensing.
- Attribution required.
- Takedown within 48 hours after written request.

## Product Implementation Notes

Hosted licensed files continue through the existing `/api/upload_video` flow in `linguid-api`.

YouTube imports should be a separate admin-only path:

- Add `POST /api/import_youtube_video`.
- Validate video IDs through YouTube Data API.
- Require `status.embeddable = true`.
- Store source metadata in Firestore.
- Use the official YouTube IFrame player in the iOS app.
- If SRT/VTT or transcript is provided, convert it into LiNGUiD captions and run the difficulty analyzer.
- If no caption data is provided, require a manual difficulty level and disable LiNGUiD tap-word captions for that video.

Firestore fields for imported videos:

- `source_type`
- `playback_provider`
- `youtube_video_id`
- `source_url`
- `creator_display_name`
- `creator_url`
- `rights_status`
- `caption_mode`
- `difficulty_source`
- `embed_status`
- `aspect_ratio`

## QA Checklist

For each content item:

- Native French speaker.
- Clear audio.
- Beginner-friendly pacing or clearly labeled level.
- No heavy slang unless labeled.
- No rights ambiguity.
- Attribution source recorded.
- Caption timing spot-checked.
- Difficulty level present.
- Content source and permission status recorded.

Weekly operations:

- Revalidate YouTube embeddability.
- Hide videos whose embeds fail.
- Track outreach conversion by source type.
- Shift time toward whichever source category replies best.
