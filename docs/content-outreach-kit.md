## Permission Ladder

Every outreach should offer these in order:

1. Official embed with attribution.
2. Permission to use or create captions/transcripts for tap-to-translate.
3. Permission to host a few short clips under the pilot license.

The first message should usually ask for levels 1 and 2 only. Bring up hosted clips only after they respond positively or if they are a studio/licensing team.

## Pre-Send Checklist

For each target:

- Watch at least 6 minutes of their content.
- Save 2 or 3 exact URLs that are slow, clear, and learner-safe.
- Note the level fit: A1, A2, B1, kids, or mixed.
- Confirm the contact route from the official site.
- Fill in the tracker row before sending.
- Attach no files on first contact unless requested.

## Conversion Notes

- Personalization matters more than length. Mention exact moments in the videos.
- Creator-teachers usually care about attribution, learner benefit, and not losing course traffic.
- Studios care about rights scope, term length, territory, brand safety, and takedown.
- The fallback ask is always official embed only.
- Do not argue if they say no. A clean no can still produce a referral.
